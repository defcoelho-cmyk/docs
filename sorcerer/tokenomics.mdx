## Core Economic Loop

\$SORX is fueled by protocol usage. Revenue generated by the product is redirected to the token via rewards, buybacks, and growth initiatives.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Usage → Revenue">
    Users interact with the product and generate revenue.\
    Usage earns points, which are converted into \$SORX only after revenue is realized.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revenue → Growth">
    A portion of the revenue is reinvested into technology and distribution.\
    More growth leads to more usage, closing the loop.
  </Step>
</Steps>
