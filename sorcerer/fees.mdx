**Sorcerer charges ≤1% per order placed.** This fee supports the development of better quantitative strategies and helps incentivize adoption through our **referral program**.

## [**​**](https://docs.polystr.at/polystrat/fees#user-incentives)**User Incentives**

The more volume you generate, the lower your fee. This is designed to attract and reward high-frequency traders on Sorcerer.

### Tier 1 — Rock

30-day volume below 5,000 SOL \
No discount \
You pay **1%** in fees 

### Tier 2 — Air

30-day volume from 5,000 SOL \
10% fee discount \
You pay **0.9%** in fees 

### Tier 3 — Fire

30-day volume from 10,000 SOL \
20% fee discount \
You pay **0.8%** in fees
