## **Revenue Share Breakdown**

Sorcerer has a 2-level referral program. You earn a percentage of Sorcerer’s net trading fee from users referred by you and by your network.[**​**](https://docs.polystr.at/polystrat/referral#referral-levels-and-commissions)

## **Referral Levels and Commissions**[**​**](https://docs.polystr.at/polystrat/referral#level-1-%E2%80%94-direct-referrals)

### **Level 1 — Direct referrals**

**30% commission**

Users who sign up using your referral link.\
You earn 30% of Sorcerer’s net fee on every trade they make.

Example

1. Your referral trades \$10,000 in one day.
2. Sorcerer fee (1%): \$100
3. Your earnings (30%): \$30[**​**](https://docs.polystr.at/polystrat/referral#level-2-%E2%80%94-referrals-of-your-referrals)

### **Level 2 — Referrals of your referrals**

**3% commission**

Users who sign up using a Level 1 referral’s link.\
You earn 3% of Sorcerer’s net fee on every trade they make.

Example

1. A Level 2 user trades \$10,000.
2. Sorcerer fee: \$100
3. Your earnings (3%): \$3
4. Level 1 referral earns: \$30[**​**](https://docs.polystr.at/polystrat/referral#level-3-%E2%80%94-referrals-of-level-2)[**​**](https://docs.polystr.at/polystrat/referral#fee-and-payout-rules)

### **Fee and Payout Rules**

Commissions are calculated from Sorcerer’s net fee.\
The base fee is 1% and may change due to discounts or promotions.

<Check>
  There is no cap on referrals or earnings. More users and more volume means higher payouts.
</Check>
