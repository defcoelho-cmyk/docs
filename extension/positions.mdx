This tab lists all your Sorcerer positions, active and inactive, with quick actions for managing them.

<img
  src="/images/positions.png"
  alt="Positions Pn"
  style={{ width:"71%" }}
/>

## Features

Quick actions to manage your positions:

| **Sell**        | Fully close the position.                    |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Share PnL**   | Create a card to share your play on socials. |
| **Hide**        | Hide the token from your panel.              |
| **Show Hidden** | Show all positions, including hidden ones.   |
