## Features

Here’s what you can manage in your profile:

| **Referral Code**         | View or change your current referral code.                                                |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Instant Trade**         | Enable or disable the Instant Trade panel for terminals like Axiom, Photon, and Terminal. |
| **Desktop Notifications** | Enable or disable desktop push notifications.                                             |
| **Wallet Settings**       | Manage your connected wallets — add, remove, or export.                                   |

Additional things you can review here:

| **Sorcerer Exp**     | Your current level on Sorcerer.                              |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Volume**           | Total volume traded using the tool.                          |
| **Referred Wallets** | Total wallets you’ve referred and the volume they generated. |
